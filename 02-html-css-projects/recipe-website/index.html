Gulab jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Mix khoya, flour and milk to make dough, shape balls, fry and soak in sugar.
  2. Prepare milk powder dough, make small balls, deep fry and dip.
  3. Make jamun with paneer and flour, fry golden and soak in cardamom sugar.
  4. Combine khoya with a little baking soda, make balls, fry and serve soaked.

Barfi jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Crush gulab jamun, mix with khoya and sugar, set and cut into barfi.
  2. Combine jamun pieces with milk solids, cook and shape into squares.
  3. Make a layer of gulab jamun and barfi mixture, chill and slice.
  4. Blend fried jamun crumbs with khoya, sugar syrup and make barfi pieces.

Kaju Katli

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Grind cashews, cook with sugar syrup, roll thin and cut into diamond shapes.
  2. Mix cashew powder and sugar syrup, add ghee, set and slice into pieces.
  3. Make kaju dough with milk and sugar, flatten and decorate with silver leaf.
  4. Cook cashew paste with sugar, knead smooth, spread on tray and cut squares.
